Contact us

You can write to the author directly through email, or report a bug on GitHub.

When reporting bugs and making feature requests, please make sure to first search in existing (opened and closed) issues.

When submitting a bug report, please, give us as much information on your issue, as you can. Which browser, what exactly you are trying to do. Send us the code you tried. The more details you give, the more likely we help. Thanks!
